<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) Detector market features various technologies, including Photoionization Detectors (PIDs) and Electrochemical Detectors. PIDs utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, offering rapid detection with high sensitivity, ideal for real-time monitoring. Electrochemical Detectors measure changes in electrical current produced by VOC reactions, providing accurate readings for specific compounds. Other detectors include thermal desorption and metal oxide semiconductor types, catering to diverse applications in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and indoor air quality assessments.</p></p>
