<p><strong>The Zinc Air Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary (Non-Rechargeable)</li><li>Secondary (Rechargeable)</li><li>Mechanical Recharge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zinc air cells are categorized into three market types: primary, secondary, and mechanical recharge. Primary (non-rechargeable) cells are single-use batteries that provide high energy density, ideal for applications like hearing aids. Secondary (rechargeable) zinc air cells can be recharged, making them suitable for devices with moderate energy needs, such as electric vehicles. Mechanical recharge systems utilize mechanical energy to regenerate the chemical reactions in zinc air batteries, enhancing sustainability by allowing multiple uses of the same cell.</p></p>
