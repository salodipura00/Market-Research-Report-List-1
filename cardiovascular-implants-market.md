<p><p>The cardiovascular implants market encompasses various devices designed to treat heart-related conditions. Implanted cardiac pacemakers regulate heartbeats, and implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs) manage life-threatening arrhythmias. Cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) devices improve heart function by coordinating contractions in heart chambers, collectively enhancing patient outcomes in cardiovascular health.</p></p>
