<p><strong>The Dental Bonding Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etch</li><li>Total-etch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bonding agent market is primarily divided into two types: self-etch and total-etch systems. Self-etch bonding agents eliminate the need for separate etching and rinsing steps, simplifying the application process while promoting strong adhesion to tooth structures. Conversely, total-etch bonding agents require an acid etching step to condition the enamel and dentin before applying the bonding agent, resulting in enhanced bond strength. Both types are essential in restorative dentistry, offering unique benefits depending on patient needs and clinical preferences.</p></p>
