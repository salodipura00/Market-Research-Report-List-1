<p><p>The Warehouse Mezzanines market consists of various types designed to optimize space and functionality. Structural mezzanines are standalone structures providing additional floors, supporting heavy loads. Catwalk mezzanines feature narrow walkways, facilitating equipment access while maximizing floor space. Perimeter mezzanines are positioned along the warehouse edges, enhancing storage capacity without consuming central space. Other types may include mobile or modular mezzanines, offering flexibility for changing operational needs. Together, these solutions improve workflow and efficiency in warehouses.</p></p>
