<p><strong>The Proton Therapy (PT) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchrotron</li><li>Cyclotron</li><li>Synchrocyclotron</li><li>Linear accelerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Proton therapy equipment includes several types of particle accelerators used in cancer treatment. Synchrotrons accelerate protons in a circular path, providing adjustable energy levels. Cyclotrons generate protons through a spiral motion at fixed energies, ideal for compact setups. Synchrocyclotrons combine features of both, allowing variable energies while maintaining a compact design. Linear accelerators (linacs) utilize electromagnetic fields to accelerate charged particles along a straight path, commonly incorporating protons for targeted tumor treatment, making them versatile in clinical settings.</p></p>
