<p><p>Cattle insurance encompasses various market types tailored to different needs. Lifetime cover provides comprehensive protection throughout an animal's life, covering illnesses and injuries but requiring regular premiums. Non-lifetime cover offers limited protection for a specific term, often excluding pre-existing conditions. Accident-only insurance focuses solely on injuries caused by accidents, making it a cost-effective option. Additionally, other market options may include customized policies addressing specific risks, such as disease outbreaks or theft, catering to diverse farming operations.</p></p>
