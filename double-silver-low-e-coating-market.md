<p><strong>The Double Silver Low-E Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrolytic low-E Coating</li><li>Sputtered low-E Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Silver Low-E Coating market includes two main types: pyrolytic and sputtered low-E coatings. Pyrolytic low-E coatings are created through a high-temperature process that bonds metal oxides to glass, offering durability and low solar heat gain. Sputtered low-E coatings, on the other hand, utilize a physical vapor deposition process to apply multiple layers of silver, enhancing energy efficiency and optical performance. Both types significantly improve thermal insulation and reduce energy costs in buildings.</p></p>
