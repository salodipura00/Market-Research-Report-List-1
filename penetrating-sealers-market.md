<p><ul><li>Silane</li><li>Siloxane</li><li>Silane Siloxane Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The penetrating sealers market is categorized into three main types: silane, siloxane, and silane siloxane mixtures. Silane sealers form a chemical bond with substrates, providing water repellency and enhancing durability, ideal for porous surfaces. Siloxane sealers, on the other hand, create a water-repellent barrier, offering protection without altering the surface appearance. Silane siloxane mixtures combine the benefits of both, ensuring deeper penetration and enhanced performance, making them suitable for a variety of applications in construction and maintenance.</p></p>
