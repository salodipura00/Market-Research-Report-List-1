<p><p>Glass wool roof insulation is widely used in both pitch and flat roof applications to enhance thermal efficiency and energy savings. In pitch roof insulation, glass wool is installed between rafters or beneath the roof structure, providing effective heat retention and soundproofing. For flat roof insulation, it is applied above or below the roof deck, improving thermal performance and preventing heat loss. Both applications ensure durability, fire resistance, and moisture control, making glass wool a versatile solution for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
