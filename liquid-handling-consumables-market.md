Needles are used for injecting or withdrawing liquids. Dispensers facilitate controlled liquid dispensing, while burettes are accurate volumetric tools for titration. Other consumables may include reservoirs and tubes, catering to diverse liquid handling needs in research and clinical settings.</p></p>
