<p><ul><li>Laser Co-Aggregation Chip Scanner</li><li>CCD Chip Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Genechip Scanner market includes two primary types: Laser Co-Aggregation Chip Scanners and CCD Chip Scanners. Laser Co-Aggregation Chip Scanners utilize laser technology to read and analyze gene expression data on microarrays, offering high sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, CCD Chip Scanners employ charge-coupled device technology to capture fluorescence from microarrays, providing a cost-effective solution with commendable imaging capabilities. Both types play crucial roles in genomic research and diagnostics, catering to diverse analytical needs in molecular biology.</p></p>
