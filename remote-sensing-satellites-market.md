<p><p>The remote sensing satellites market comprises three main types: SATCOM, Space-Based Radars, and Space-Based EO/IR. SATCOM satellites facilitate communication by providing data relay and broadcasting services. Space-Based Radars are utilized for surveillance and reconnaissance, offering high-resolution imaging and tracking capabilities. Space-Based EO/IR focuses on Earth observation through optical and infrared sensors, enabling environmental monitoring, disaster management, and resource assessment. Together, these technologies enhance our understanding of the Earth and support various applications across industries.</p></p>
