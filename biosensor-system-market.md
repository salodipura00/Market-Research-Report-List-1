<p><p>The biosensor system market encompasses various types, including thermal, electrochemical, and optical biosensors. Thermal biosensors detect changes in temperature due to biochemical reactions, offering sensitivity in various applications. Electrochemical biosensors operate by measuring electrical signals resulting from biochemical interactions, making them ideal for detecting metabolites and pathogens. Optical biosensors utilize light-based technologies to detect biological activities, providing real-time monitoring capabilities. Together, these types cater to healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety, driving advancements in diagnostics and analysis.</p></p>
