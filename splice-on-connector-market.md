<p><p>The splice market for connectors can be categorized into single-mode and multimode types. Single-mode connector splices are designed for high-bandwidth, long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and maximize performance. In contrast, multimode connector splices accommodate multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances and higher data throughput in local area networks. Both market types are essential in fiber optic systems, serving diverse applications across telecommunications and data networks.</p></p>
