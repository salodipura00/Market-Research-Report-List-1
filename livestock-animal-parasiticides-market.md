<p><p>The livestock animal parasiticides market is segmented into three main types: endoparasiticides, ectoparasiticides, and endectocides. Endoparasiticides target internal parasites such as worms and protozoa, effectively protecting livestock from infections that can impact growth and health. Ectoparasiticides address external parasites like fleas, ticks, and mites, ensuring animal comfort and reducing disease transmission. Endectocides combine the effects of both, treating both internal and external parasites simultaneously, offering comprehensive protection and simplifying treatment protocols for livestock producers.</p></p>
