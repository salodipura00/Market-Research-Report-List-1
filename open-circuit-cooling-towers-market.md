<p><p>Open circuit cooling towers are essential for dissipating heat in industrial processes and HVAC systems. The market primarily features two types: counterflow and crossflow cooling towers. Counterflow towers allow air to flow vertically upward against the downward-moving water, enhancing efficiency and reducing footprint. In contrast, crossflow towers have water flowing horizontally across the air stream, promoting better heat exchange and ease of maintenance. Both types cater to varying operational needs, influencing market dynamics and applications across different industries.</p></p>
