<p><strong>The Mineral Insulated Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral insulated thermocouple market is categorized into three types: grounded, ungrounded, and exposed. Grounded thermocouples provide rapid response times by having a direct contact between the sensing element and the metal sheath, making them suitable for dynamic temperature measurements. Ungrounded types offer electrical isolation, preventing interference from external signals, ideal for high-voltage applications. Exposed thermocouples feature a bare sensing element, allowing for quick temperature readings in environments where rapid changes occur, but may lack protection from mechanical damage.</p></p>
