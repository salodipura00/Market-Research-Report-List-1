<p><p>The road sweeper machines market comprises three main types: mechanical sweepers, vacuum sweepers, and regenerative air sweepers. Mechanical sweepers utilize rotating brushes to lift debris, making them suitable for moderate dirt removal. Vacuum sweepers employ suction mechanisms to collect dust and litter, ideal for urban areas requiring thorough cleaning. Regenerative air sweepers combine both air and brush systems, allowing for deep cleaning of various surfaces while minimizing dust emissions, making them efficient for larger-scale urban and highway applications.</p></p>
