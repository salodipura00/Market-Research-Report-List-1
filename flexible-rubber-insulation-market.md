<p><p>The Flexible Rubber Insulation market is categorized mainly into NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based, and Chloroprene based types, alongside other specialty materials. NBR offers excellent temperature resistance and oil resistance, making it ideal for HVAC applications. EPDM is favored for its ozone and weather resistance, suitable for outdoor uses. Chloroprene, known for its flexibility and strength, is often used in marine and refrigeration sectors. Other materials may include PVC or silicone, catering to specific insulation needs.</p></p>
