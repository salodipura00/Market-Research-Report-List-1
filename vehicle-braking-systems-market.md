<p><ul><li>Drum Brake</li><li>Disc Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle braking systems market comprises primarily two types: drum brakes and disc brakes. Drum brakes utilize a cylindrical design where brake shoes press against the inner surface of a rotating drum, providing effective stopping power and often lower manufacturing costs. Disc brakes, featuring a flat, circular disc where calipers squeeze brake pads to create friction, offer superior heat dissipation, improved performance, and responsiveness. The choice between these systems depends on factors like vehicle type, performance requirements, and cost considerations.</p></p>
