<p><p>Vapor Recovery Units (VRUs) are essential for capturing and recycling hydrocarbon vapors during the storage and transfer of liquids. The market is divided into two main types: open-type recovery and closed-type recovery. </p><p>Open-type recovery systems capture vapors exposed to the atmosphere, allowing some emissions. In contrast, closed-type recovery systems efficiently contain and recirculate vapors, minimizing environmental impact and improving safety. Both systems serve industries like oil and gas, aiming to enhance operational efficiency and comply with regulatory standards.</p></p>
