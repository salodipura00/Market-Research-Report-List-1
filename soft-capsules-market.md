<p><p>The soft capsules market is primarily categorized into gelatin soft capsules and vegetarian soft capsules. Gelatin soft capsules are made from animal-derived gelatin, offering an efficient delivery system for various formulations, such as vitamins and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, vegetarian soft capsules are created from plant-based materials like pullulan or hydroxypropyl methylcellulose, catering to vegan and vegetarian consumers. Both types provide advantages in terms of bioavailability, ease of swallowing, and taste masking, thereby enhancing the overall consumer experience.</p></p>
