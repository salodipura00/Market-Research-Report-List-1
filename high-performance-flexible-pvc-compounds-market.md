<p><p>The High Performance Flexible PVC Compounds market is divided into two primary types: Non-Plasticised PVC (NPVC) and Plasticised PVC (PPVC). NPVC is characterized by its rigidity and resistance to chemical and UV damage, making it suitable for applications requiring durability without flexibility. Conversely, PPVC incorporates plasticisers, enhancing flexibility, processability, and impact resistance, making it ideal for products like cables, films, and medical devices. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on their intended applications and performance requirements.</p></p>
