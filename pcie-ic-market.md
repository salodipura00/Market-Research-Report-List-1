<p><p>The PCIe IC market consists of various types, notably PCIe Switch ICs and PCIe Signal Enhancement ICs. PCIe Switch ICs facilitate multiple device connections to a single PCIe bus, optimizing data traffic and improving bandwidth utilization. In contrast, PCIe Signal Enhancement ICs boost signal integrity and extend transmission distances, which is critical for high-speed data transfer in complex systems. Together, these components enhance the performance and reliability of PCIe interfaces across diverse applications, including data centers and consumer electronics.</p></p>
