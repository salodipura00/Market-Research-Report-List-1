<p><p>The High Pressure Valve Stem Seals market primarily features two types: Positive Seals and Umbrella Seals. Positive Seals are designed to provide a tight seal against pressure, preventing leaks and ensuring optimal functionality in high-stress environments. Umbrella Seals, often shaped like an umbrella, also prevent fluid leakage but allow for better lubrication and accommodation of movement. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive and industrial applications, to maintain performance and reliability under high-pressure conditions.</p></p>
