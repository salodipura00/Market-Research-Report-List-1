<p><p>The fire sprinkler system market includes three main types: wet, dry, and deluge systems. Wet sprinkler systems contain water in the pipes, providing immediate activation during a fire. Dry sprinkler systems have air or nitrogen in the pipes, releasing water only when triggered, ideal for cold environments. Deluge systems have open sprinklers connected to a water supply, releasing water simultaneously when activated, suitable for high hazard areas. Each system serves specific fire protection needs across various industries.</p></p>
