<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spiral Welded Pipes and Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Side Welding</li><li>Double Side Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spiral welded pipes and tubes are produced by winding a strip of metal and welding along the seam, providing strength and flexibility. The market is segmented into single-side and double-side welding types. Single-side welding involves applying a weld bead to one side of the spiral seam, suitable for low-pressure applications. In contrast, double-side welding features welds on both sides, enhancing durability and pressure resistance, making it ideal for high-stress environments, such as oil and gas pipelines.</p></p>
