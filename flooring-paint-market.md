<p><strong>The Flooring Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flooring paint market is segmented into waterborne and solventborne types. Waterborne paints use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly, low-VOC (volatile organic compound) solutions with quick drying times and easy cleanup. They are ideal for indoor applications due to minimal odor. Solventborne paints use chemical solvents, providing superior durability, adhesion, and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for high-traffic or outdoor areas. However, they emit higher VOCs and require proper ventilation. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing environmental concerns and performance requirements.</p></p>
