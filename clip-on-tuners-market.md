<p><p>Clip-on tuners are categorized into three market types based on their tuning performance: Flat, In Tune, and Sharp. Flat tuners indicate that the pitch is below the desired note, requiring the musician to raise their tuning. In Tune tuners show that the instrument is perfectly pitched, offering a green light or indicator for confidence in performance. Sharp tuners signal that the pitch exceeds the target note, prompting the musician to lower their tuning for optimal sound. Each type aids musicians in achieving precise intonation.</p></p>
