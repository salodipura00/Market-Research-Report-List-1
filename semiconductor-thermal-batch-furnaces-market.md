<p><p>The semiconductor thermal batch furnaces market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal type furnaces are designed to accommodate wafers placed flat in a horizontal orientation, offering uniform heat distribution and easy loading/unloading. In contrast, vertical type furnaces position wafers in a vertical stack, which can optimize space and improve thermal efficiency. Both types serve critical functions in processes like oxidation, diffusion, and annealing, catering to diverse manufacturing needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
