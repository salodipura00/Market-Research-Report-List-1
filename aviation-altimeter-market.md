<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aviation Altimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barometric Altimeter</li><li>Radio Altimeter</li><li>GNSS (GPS, Galileo, etc).</li><li>Laser Altimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation altimeter market consists of several key types. Barometric altimeters measure altitude based on atmospheric pressure changes, commonly used in aircraft. Radio altimeters utilize radio signals to determine precise altitude above the ground, enhancing safety during low visibility conditions. GNSS altimeters rely on satellite systems like GPS and Galileo for accurate altitude data, complementing traditional methods. Laser altimeters use laser pulses to measure distance from the aircraft to the ground, providing high-resolution altitude data, particularly in advanced applications.</p></p>
