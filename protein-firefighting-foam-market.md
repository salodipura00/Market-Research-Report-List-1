<p><p>Protein firefighting foam is a specialized type of foam used to combat flammable liquid fires, such as those involving petroleum, oils, and other hydrocarbons. This foam is biodegradable, environmentally friendly, and highly effective in fire suppression, making it a preferred choice in industries like oil and gas, aviation, and petrochemicals.</p><p>The Protein Firefighting Foam Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
