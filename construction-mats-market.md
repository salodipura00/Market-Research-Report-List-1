<p><p>The construction mats market comprises three primary types: composite mats, wood mats, and metal mats. Composite mats are engineered from materials like high-density polyethylene, offering durability and lightweight properties suited for various terrains. Wood mats, typically made from hardwood, provide strength and are often used for temporary roadways and stabilization. Metal mats, usually constructed from steel or aluminum, offer superior load-bearing capabilities and are ideal for heavy equipment use. Each type is tailored to specific construction site requirements, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
