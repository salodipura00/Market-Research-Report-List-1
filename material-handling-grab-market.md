<p><p>The Material Handling Grab market is primarily divided into hydraulic and mechanical types. Hydraulic grabs utilize fluid power to operate, offering precision and efficiency in lifting heavy loads. They are commonly used in demanding environments such as construction and marine applications. In contrast, mechanical grabs rely on a series of levers and gears to function, providing a more straightforward and often cost-effective solution for material handling tasks. Both types cater to varying operational needs and performance expectations across industries.</p></p>
