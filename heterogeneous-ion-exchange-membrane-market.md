<p><strong>The Heterogeneous Ion-exchange Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Membrane</li><li>Bipolar Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heterogeneous ion-exchange membrane market includes unipolar and bipolar membranes, each serving distinct applications. Unipolar membranes facilitate the selective transport of either cations or anions, commonly used in water treatment, desalination, and electrochemical processes. In contrast, bipolar membranes enable the simultaneous movement of both cations and anions, allowing for the generation of acids and bases through electrochemical reactions. This makes bipolar membranes ideal for application in energy storage, fuel cells, and innovative separation processes, enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
