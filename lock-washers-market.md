<p><p>The lock washers market is categorized into external and internal types. External lock washers, like star washers, provide a larger surface area for better grip and are often used in situations where additional locking force is required. Internal star washers, on the other hand, fit inside the head of a screw or bolt, offering a secure fit to prevent loosening. Split washers are a popular variation, featuring a split design that creates tension during installation. Other types include serrated and toothed washers, each serving specific applications.</p></p>
