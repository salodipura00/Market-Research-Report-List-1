<p><p>Peristaltic tube pumps are a type of positive displacement pump that move fluids through flexible tubing. They operate by compressing and relaxing the tube, creating a vacuum that draws fluid in and then pushes it out as the tube returns to its original shape. This mechanism allows for precise flow control and minimal risk of contamination, making them ideal for applications in pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and wastewater management.</p><p>The Peristaltic Tube Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.2% during the forecast period, driven by advancements in technology and increasing demand for efficient fluid handling solutions. <p><p>Peristaltic tube pumps are categorized into fixed speed and variable speed markets. Fixed speed pumps operate at a constant rotational speed, providing reliable, consistent flow for applications where precise control is less critical. Conversely, variable speed pumps allow for adjustable flow rates, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in processes requiring diverse fluid handling. This adaptability makes variable speed pumps suitable for applications with varying demands, catering to industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and wastewater treatment, where precision and responsiveness are essential.</p></p>
