<p><p>The sound bar market is divided into active and passive categories. Active sound bars incorporate built-in amplifiers and sound processing, allowing them to function independently and deliver high-quality audio without additional components. They are user-friendly and ideal for casual listeners. Conversely, passive sound bars require external amplification and are often used in more sophisticated audio setups. These typically offer better sound customization and integration with other audio equipment, appealing to audiophiles seeking superior performance.</p></p>
