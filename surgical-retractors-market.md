<p><ul><li>Hand Held Retractors</li><li>Self-Retaining Retractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical retractors market is categorized into two main types: hand-held retractors and self-retaining retractors. Hand-held retractors require manual assistance from surgical staff to hold back tissues and expose surgical sites, providing flexibility and control during procedures. Self-retaining retractors, on the other hand, are designed to hold themselves in place, allowing surgeons to focus on the operation without needing additional assistance. Both types are crucial for enhancing visibility and access during surgeries, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
