<p><p>The seat belt buckle sensor market is segmented into three main types based on the safety belt configuration: two-point, three-point, and four-point safety belts. Two-point safety belts typically consist of a lap belt that secures the occupant at the waist. Three-point safety belts add a shoulder strap, enhancing safety by distributing force in a crash.
