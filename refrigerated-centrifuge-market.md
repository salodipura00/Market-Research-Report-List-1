<p><p>The refrigerated centrifuge market is divided into two main types: low-speed and high-speed refrigerated centrifuges. Low-speed refrigerated centrifuges operate at lower RPMs, making them suitable for separating larger particles and maintaining sample integrity for sensitive biological applications. In contrast, high-speed refrigerated centrifuges operate at higher RPMs, allowing for rapid separation of smaller particles and increased efficiency in processing samples. Both types are essential in laboratories for various applications, including cell culture and molecular biology.</p></p>
