<p><p>The 3D water cutting machine market is primarily segmented into two types: sandless cutting and abrasive cutting. Sandless cutting utilizes high-pressure water jets to create precise cuts in materials without the need for abrasives, making it ideal for softer materials like plastics and food products. In contrast, abrasive cutting incorporates granulate materials into the water jet, enabling it to cut through harder substances like metal and stone. Both methods offer unique advantages for various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and precision in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
