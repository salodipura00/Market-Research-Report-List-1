<p><p>The Chlorinated Polyvinylchloride (CPVC) compound market is categorized into two primary types: High Chlorine and Low Chlorine. High Chlorine CPVC contains a higher percentage of chlorine, enhancing its thermal and chemical resistance, making it suitable for high-temperature applications like piping systems. In contrast, Low Chlorine CPVC contains a reduced chlorine content, offering improved flexibility and better processing characteristics, making it ideal for applications that require moderate temperature resistance and ease of fabrication, such as fittings and valves.</p></p>
