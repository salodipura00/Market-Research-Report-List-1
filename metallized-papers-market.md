<p><ul><li>Lamination Type</li><li>Vaccum Metallization Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallized papers market is segmented by lamination and vacuum metallization types. Lamination type involves bonding a thin metal foil to paper, offering durability, high gloss, and enhanced barrier properties, ideal for packaging and labeling. Vacuum metallization type uses a process where metal is vaporized and deposited onto paper, creating a reflective, lightweight, and cost-effective finish, commonly used in decorative and flexible packaging. Both types provide aesthetic appeal, moisture resistance, and improved shelf life, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
