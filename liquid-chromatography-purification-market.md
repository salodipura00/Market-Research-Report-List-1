<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography Purification market can be categorized into two main types: single-step purification and multiple-step purification. Single-step purification typically involves isolating a target compound in one process, making it efficient and straightforward for applications requiring high purity without complex procedures. In contrast, multiple-step purification entails a series of sequential processes to achieve greater purity or separation of compounds, offering enhanced specificity and yield for complex mixtures. Both methods cater to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research.</p></p>
