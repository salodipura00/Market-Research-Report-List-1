<p><p>The steam coal market is primarily categorized into two types: lump steam coal and steam coal fines. Lump steam coal consists of larger, cohesive chunks ideal for industrial purposes, such as electricity generation and heating. In contrast, steam coal fines consist of smaller particles, often arising from the processing of lump coal. Fines are typically used in applications requiring lower energy density or can be blended with lump coal. Both types serve essential roles in energy production and industrial operations.</p></p>
