<p><p>The Linear Variable Displacement Transformer (LVDT) market is segmented into AC and DC types based on the signal output. AC LVDTs utilize alternating current to generate a voltage output proportional to the displacement, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. They are widely used in industrial and aerospace applications. Conversely, DC LVDTs operate on direct current, providing more straightforward signal processing and compatibility with digital systems. Both types serve critical roles in automation, measurement, and control systems across various industries.</p></p>
