<p><p>Intelligent Flexible Systems (IFS) cater to various manufacturing needs through distinct market types. Sequential FMS operates in a predetermined order, optimizing workflow for specific tasks. Random FMS allows dynamic scheduling, accommodating unexpected changes in production demand. Dedicated FMS is tailored for specific products, enhancing efficiency but lacking versatility. Engineered FMS focuses on highly customized systems for complex manufacturing processes. Modular FMS offers interchangeable components, facilitating quick adjustments to production lines and improving adaptability to changing product requirements.</p></p>
