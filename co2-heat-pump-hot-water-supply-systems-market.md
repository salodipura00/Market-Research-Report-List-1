<p><p>CO2 heat pump hot water supply systems can be categorized into three main types: air source, water source, and other sources. Air source systems extract heat from the ambient air to heat water, making them versatile for various climates. Water source systems utilize heat from bodies of water, ensuring high efficiency, particularly in coastal or lake-adjacent locations. Other types may include ground source systems that leverage geothermal energy or hybrid systems combining multiple heat sources for enhanced performance and reliability.</p></p>
