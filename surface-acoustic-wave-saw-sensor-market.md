<p><ul><li>Resonators</li><li>Delay Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensor market encompasses two primary types: resonators and delay lines. Resonators are used to create precise frequency signals, applicable in sensing temperature, pressure, and gas. They leverage the resonant frequency shift due to environmental changes. Delay lines, on the other hand, are utilized for phase shift measurements. They detect variations in parameters like humidity and chemical composition by analyzing the time delay of the acoustic wave. Both types are critical for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
