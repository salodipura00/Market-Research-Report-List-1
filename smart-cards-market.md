<p><p>The smart card market encompasses various types, including One-Time Password (OTP) display cards and Multifactor Authentication (MFA) display cards. OTP display cards generate temporary passwords for secure access, enhancing user authentication during online transactions. MFA display cards add an additional layer of security by requiring multiple forms of verification, such as a password and a biometric scan. Both types address growing cybersecurity concerns, providing robust solutions for identity verification in sectors like banking, healthcare, and corporate environments.</p></p>
